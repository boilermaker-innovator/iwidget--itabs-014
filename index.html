WA's premier startup accelerator program, Plus Eight Accelerator

Plus Eight Accelerator

Western Australia's premier startup accelerator program, running since 2017.

  • Supported 45+ startups to date
  • Helped raise over $29M in follow-on funding
  • 12-week intensive mentorship program
  • Access to global network of mentors and investors

The program is a partnership between Spacecubed and several corporate partners committed to innovation in WA.

, is back for 2025 and now accepting applications from ambitious founders ready to scale their businesses globally.

Selected startups will receive up to $400,000 investment

Investment Structure

The $400,000 investment is structured as:

  • $200,000 initial investment at program start
  • $200,000 follow-on upon successful program completion
  • Standard terms: 7-10% equity stake
  • SAFE notes available for early-stage startups

This investment structure is competitive with leading accelerators globally while being tailored to the Australian market.

to accelerate their growth, alongside mentorship from industry leaders and a structured 12-week program designed to take their business to the next level.

  • Runs multiple accelerator and incubator programs
  • 10,000+ community members engaged annually

Spacecubed's mission is to provide the physical spaces, digital platforms, and startup programs to support 100,000 entrepreneurs by 2030.

Corporate Partners

RAC: Western Australia's leading member organization providing roadside, insurance, travel and finance services to over 1 million members.

Who Should Apply?

Applications are open to startups across Australia, with a focus on those that have demonstrated product-market fit and are ready to scale. The program is particularly interested in innovations in sustainability, health tech, and enterprise solutions

Focus Industries

Sustainability: Climate tech, renewable energy, circular economy, carbon reduction solutions, and sustainable materials.

Health Tech: Digital health, remote monitoring, health data analytics, wellness applications, and medical technology.

Enterprise Solutions: B2B software, AI applications, automation, logistics optimization, and industry-specific productivity tools.

While these are focus areas, exceptional startups from any sector are encouraged to apply.

"Plus Eight is the rocket fuel for startups that are ready to grow quickly. We're not just providing investment, but a complete ecosystem to support that growth."

The Program Experience

The 12-week accelerator program includes:

  • Weekly workshops with industry experts
  • One-on-one mentoring sessions
  • Customer introduction opportunities
  • Investor readiness preparation
  • Co-working space at Spacecubed locations
  • Demo Day presentation to investors and partners

Application Process

The application process for Plus Eight 2025 involves:

  1. Initial online application
  2. Shortlisted team interviews
  3. Final selection by investment committee
